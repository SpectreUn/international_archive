What is the Spectre Initative? +

The Spectre International Archive is a digital repository of declassified documents and historical records related to international relations and global diplomacy.

How can I access documents in the archive? +

Most documents in our archive are freely accessible to the public. Simply navigate to the archive section and browse or search for documents of interest.

Are the documents in the archive verified? +

Yes, all documents undergo a verification process before being added to the archive. We work with historians and experts to authenticate materials.

Can I contribute to the archive? +

We welcome contributions from researchers and the public. Please contact us through our submission form with details about the materials you'd like to contribute.

How often is the archive updated? +

We add new materials regularly as they become available and pass our verification process. Major updates are announced on our homepage.

Is there a way to request specific documents? +

Yes, you can submit document requests through our contact page. While we can't guarantee fulfillment, we'll do our best to locate relevant materials.
